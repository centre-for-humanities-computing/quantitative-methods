Exercise 2: Case Study Application

Objective

Apply insights from the presented case study to your own research, exploring practical uses of computational and quantitative methods.

Instructions

Step 1 (5 min):

Review your earlier research description and the quantitative methods you identified in Exercise 1.

Step 2 (10 min):

Reflect on how the approaches and outcomes demonstrated in the case study presented by Mia Jacobsen apply to or inform your own research.

Step 3 (20 min):

In groups of 2-3, discuss specific ways the case study methods can practically inform or enhance your research projects. Provide mutual feedback on practical implementation and potential challenges.

Expected Outcome

By completing this exercise, you will develop concrete strategies for applying case study insights to your own research, enhanced by peer discussions and targeted instructor support.
