Exercise 3: Challenges to Quantification

Objective

Reflect critically on the limitations and challenges associated with applying quantitative and computational methods to your research.

Instructions

Step 1 (5 min):

List potential limitations or challenges you foresee when applying quantitative methods to your specific research area or topic.

Step 2 (10 min):

Discuss in pairs how these identified challenges might affect the validity, interpretability, or ethical implications of your research.
