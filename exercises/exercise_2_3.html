Objective

In this exercise, you will identify the advantages and disadvantages of using the command line and how to learn how to use it for data triage.

Feel free to open a command line, if you have access to one.

Instructions

Step 1 (15 min): War stories

In this exercise, you will discuss among your group the advantages and disadvantages of using the command line and you experiences with the command line or GUI programs in relation to the problems laid out in the live coding session.

Discuss the following issues:

  • Experience with unknown file formats, and how they were overcome
  • Experience with character encodings, and how they were overcome
  • Experience with broken files and file formats, and how they were overcome
  • Experience with similiar problems and how they were overcome

Step 2 (10 min): Next steps

Were there things in the live session, that did not make sense or was trivial?

Discuss how to proceed in becoming familiar with the command line. Where could you go to find information, tutorials, help?

  • If on a Windows PC

Step 3 (5 min): Do you think, you will ever use the command line?

If not, why? ;-)

Expected Outcome

you should now have a beginning of an idea of what the command line might be capable of and a beginning understanding of files.

You should also have an idea of where and how to take the next steps.
